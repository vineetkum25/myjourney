My Professional Journey

2019 – Completed B.B.A. Degree

Graduated with a Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.).

2020 – Developed Voucher Card Sharing Software

  • Hired a software agency to create a digital voucher card platform.
  • Due to the COVID-19 lockdown, product development and market testing were delayed. Once the lockdown was lifted, I tested the product directly with retail shop owners.
  • Although the product didn’t gain market traction, the experience provided valuable insights into user needs and product-market fit.

2021-2023 – Focused on Programming & Technology related skills.

  • Realized that mastering programming was essential for turning my startup ideas into reality and could also serve as a backup for launching a software development agency.
  • Dedicated these years to learning programming, web development, mobile app development, and other key technical skills.
  • Worked on two forum websites to practice programming and test their market potential:
    • First Forum Website: Created a forum where users could access discussion pages by typing in the industry name and region name. This allowed professionals to connect based on their industry and location.
    • Second Forum Website: Built a forum where users could embed their social media posts directly into the forum pages, enabling them to showcase and promote their content and social media profiles.
  • Both forum websites failed to gain user interest and engagement during the testing phase with a small group of users. However, working on these projects greatly increased my confidence in programming as I gained hands-on experience with real-world applications.

2024 – Launching a New Tech Startup

With advanced programming expertise, hands-on experience in web and mobile development, and a deeper understanding of product development, I’m now preparing to launch my next exciting tech venture.
